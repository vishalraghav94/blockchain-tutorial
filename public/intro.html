Introduction
Objective of this tutorial
This tutorial aims to provide the reader with a basic understanding of Blockchain Technology and Decentralised Apps. This tutorial consist of step by step understanding of, how to create a decentralised app using in-memory blockchain and aims to extend to the real Ethereum Blockchain.
This tutorial will teach you following things:
  • Basic understanding of blockchain
  • Why blockchain is secure?
  • What are the applications of blockchain?
  • What is Smart Contract?
  • What is a DApp?
  • How to code a smart contract in solidity language?
  • Creating a Node.js based Dapp to vote for a candidate.
  • Using Angular.js to create the frontend of the Dapp.
What is a Blockchain?
You will find definition of Blockchain on internet very easily, so I will not go into that, instead I highly recommend you to watch the video first. The video clearly explain basic of Blockchain, thanks to Mr. Shai Rubin for creating such an awesome video.
Now, that you got the idea of Blockchain (Assuming that you’ve watched the video), you should be able to answer these questions, and if you are not then watch it again :
  • One transaction happens and will get recorded into the ledger,but how will that transactions be validated and who will do that ?
  • Lets say you’ve $1 and you sent it to someone and before it is getting validated and added to the ledger by miner, you did another transaction with that $1 (i.e. Double spending problem), then what will happen?
  • Miners do validation of transaction — but what is validation and what does it means?
